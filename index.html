Project Farmoxel

The Farmoxel Project (This is a placeholder name, don't get too attached to it), is a resource management farming game in a voxel art style highly inspired in games like 'Stardew Valley' and 'Slime Rancher'. Our main goal is to understand where these games succeeded and where they failed, and try to solve their mistakes.

Room Makers

Room Makers is the story of a very lucky guy who is receiving tons of quality furniture for his new home. Hurry up and use your previously acquired Tetris abilities to help him get his job done. Move furniture, make combos, get points and buy new rooms to get the house of your dreams!

KTA

KTA is a procedurally generated game where you control a spaceship, and the only purpose is to make the most points you can.
